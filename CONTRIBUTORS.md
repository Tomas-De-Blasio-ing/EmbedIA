# EmbedIA Authors and Contributors

This project is developed within the framework of the
Instituto de Investigación en Informática LIDI (III-LIDI),
School of Computer Science, National University of La Plata (UNLP).

## Main authors
- **César Estrebou** – Design, main development, documentation.
- **Marcos David Saavedra** – Design, main development, documentation.

This project was made possible through the collaboration of faculty advisors and 
students participating in academic innovation and professional training programs.

### Student Contributors

The following students contributed to this project during their 
**Supervised Professional Practice (SPP)** and/or through participation in 
**Student-Led Innovation Projects (SLIP)**. Their work included research, 
prototyping, testing, documentation and software development in python and C.

- Marcos David Saavedra - SPP & SLIP – Tensorflow Basic Layers
- Martín Fleming - SPP & SLIP - Depthwise Separable Convolution Layer 
- Federico Adra - SPP & SLIP – Project Converter
- Yair Maximiliano Ciprés – SPP - Binary Neural Networks
- Luciano Esteban Thea – SPP - Batch Normalization Layer
- Valentina Madonia - SPP - Support Vector Machine
- Nicolás Guerrero Agustín - SPP - K Nearest Neighbors
- Gabriel Di Marco - SPP - Decision Trees

We gratefully acknowledge the dedication and effort of all participants.
